Meet New York City's 2021 mayoral candidates

By Olivia Bensimon and Jo Constantz | March 10, 2021

Over two dozen candidates are vying for New York City's top spot, all looking to replace Mayor Bill de Blasio. With such a crowded field, it can be difficult to figure out who to support.

Here are the top contenders. You can filter by party, professional background, and whether they are a cityborn New Yorker. To learn more about a candidate, click on their card to visit their campaign website.

Scott Stringer, D

Born: New York City, N.Y.

Background: Government

Scott Stringer held his first public office at age 16, serving on a community board in Manhattan. He's now the city's comptroller.

Eric Adams, D

Born: Brownsville, New York, N.Y.

Background: Government

Eric Adams served as a New York City police officer for over two decades, and now serves as the Brooklyn Borough President.

Maya Wiley, D

Born: Syracuse, N.Y.

Background: Government

Maya Wiley, a former political commentator on MSNBC, has served as the chairwoman of the Civilian Complaint Review Board and as counsel to Mayor Bill de Blasio.

Shaun Donovan, D

Born: New York City, N.Y.

Background: Government

Shaun Donovan is a former federal housing secretary under the Obama administration and a former White House budget director.

Kathryn Garcia, D

Born: Brooklyn, N.Y.

Background: Government

Kathryn Garcia served as the sanitation commissioner for New York City.

Ray McGuire, D

Born: Dayton, O.H.

Background: Finance

Raymond McGuire is a former vice-chair of Citigroup, an investment bank.

Loree Sutton, D

Born: Loma Linda, C.A.

Carlos Menchaca, D

Born: El Paso, T.X.

Background: Government

Carlos Menchaca is a City Council member for district 38 in Brooklyn, serving Sunset Park and Red Hook.

Dianne Morales, D

Born: Brooklyn, N.Y.

Background: Nonprofit

Dianne Morales served as the executive director of Phipps Neighborhoods, a social-services organization in the Bronx.

Curtis Sliwa, R

Born: Brooklyn, N.Y.

Background: Entertainment

Curtis Sliwa, a radio talkshow host, founded the Guardian Angels, a crime-prevention group, in 1979.

Andrew Yang, D

Born: Schenectady, N.Y.

Background: Business

Andrew Yang is an entreprenuer, nonprofit founder, and former presidential candidate.

Barbara Kavovit, D

Born: Bronx, N.Y.

Background: Business

Barbara Kavovit is a founder of a construction firm and a reality TV personality, known for her appearance on "The Real Housewives of New York City."

Fernando Mateo, R

Born: The Dominican Republic

Background: Business

Fernando Mateo is a restaurant owner and has led trade groups for cabdrivers, delivery drivers, and bodega workers.

Sara Tirschwell, R

Born: Corpus Christi, T.X.

Background: Finance

Sara Tirschwell is a former hedge fund manager, known for filing the first "Me Too" sexual harassment complaint on Wall Street.
